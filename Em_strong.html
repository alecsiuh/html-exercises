Nexus 5

The Nexus 5 (2015) will be unveiled on Tuesday, September 29 according to a report from CNET. CNET does not provide
a source for this information, but recent, unrelated speculation also suggested this date. CNET also adds weight to the rumors
that two devices will be revealed at this launch; the smaller Nexus made by LG and the larger version by Huawei.

This news might have inadvertently revealed the release date for Android Marshmallow too. Google usually drops its latest Nexus devices at the same time as the latest
version of Android, as it did last year with the Nexus 6 and Android 5.0 Lollipop.
Will we see this trend continue in 2015? It won't be long until we find out.
